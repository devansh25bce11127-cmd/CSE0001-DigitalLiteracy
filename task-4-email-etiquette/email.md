### Email 1: Requesting an Assignment Deadline Extension

Subject: Request for Extension on Assignment Submission – Transform Techniques

Dear Sir/Madam
I hope you are doing well. I am writing to respectfully request a short extension for the submission of my assignment for the course [Course Name], which is currently due on [original due date].
Over the past few days, I have been facing some unforeseen circumstances that have affected my ability to complete the assignment on time. Despite my efforts to stay on schedule, I believe that a brief extension would allow me to finish the work properly and submit a more complete and well-prepared assignment.
If possible, I kindly request an extension until [new requested date]. I assure you that I am committed to completing the assignment and maintaining the quality of my work.
Thank you very much for your understanding and consideration. Please let me know if any additional information is required.

Sincerely,  
Devansh Varshney (25BCE11127)

### Email 2: Inquiry About a Summer Internship Opportunity

Subject: Inquiry Regarding Summer Internship Opportunities

Dear Internship Coordinator,
I hope this message finds you well. My name is Devansh, and I am currently a Computer Science Engineering student. I am writing to express my interest in potential summer internship opportunities at your organization.
I am particularly interested in gaining practical experience in areas such as software development, backend systems, and DevOps practices. During my studies, I have been developing my skills in programming languages such as Python and C++, along with basic experience in SQL and web development.
I am eager to apply my academic knowledge in a professional environment while learning from experienced professionals in the industry. I believe that an internship at your organization would provide valuable exposure to real-world software development and technology practices.
I would greatly appreciate the opportunity to learn more about any available internship programs for the upcoming summer. Please let me know if you require my resume or any additional information.
Thank you for your time and consideration. I look forward to hearing from you.

Best regards,  
Devansh
