# Responsible Social Media Use: Do's and Don'ts Checklist

| **Do's (Responsible Practices)** | **Don'ts (Practices to Avoid)** |
|----------------------------------|---------------------------------|
| Use strong privacy settings to control who can see your posts and personal information. | Do not share personal details such as your home address, phone number, or financial information publicly. |
| Think carefully before posting content to ensure it is respectful and appropriate. | Avoid posting offensive, abusive, or discriminatory content. |
| Share useful, educational, or positive content that reflects your personality and interests. | Do not spread misinformation or share unverified news or rumors. |
| Maintain a professional online presence, especially on platforms like LinkedIn. | Avoid posting content that could damage your academic or professional reputation. |
| Report suspicious accounts or harmful behavior to the platform administrators. | Do not engage in cyberbullying, harassment, or online arguments. |
