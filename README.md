<h2>🔐 Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness</h2>

<h3>Part A – Cybercrime Case Study</h3>

<p>
A detailed case study was written on a specific type of cybercrime.
The case study explains how the cybercrime occurs, who is usually targeted,
and the consequences for victims.
</p>

<p>
The selected cybercrime topic includes examples such as:</p>

<ul>
<li>Phishing</li>
<li>UPI / Online Payment Fraud</li>
<li>Identity Theft</li>
<li>Cyberbullying</li>
<li>Ransomware</li>
</ul>

<p>
The case study contains approximately <b>200–250 words</b> and describes:</p>

<ul>
<li>What the cybercrime is</li>
<li>How it typically occurs (step-by-step)</li>
<li>Who the common victims are</li>
<li>The possible consequences</li>
</ul>

<p>
The document is stored in the repository inside the folder <b>task-5-cybercrime/</b>
as <b>casestudy.md</b> or <b>casestudy.pdf</b>.
</p>

<h3>Part B – Stay Safe Online Checklist</h3>

<p>
A safety checklist was created to help students protect themselves
from cybercrime while using the internet.
</p>

<ul>
<li>Includes at least <b>8 practical safety tips</b></li>
<li>At least <b>2 tips related to financial or UPI safety</b></li>
<li>Provides official cybercrime reporting resources</li>
</ul>

<p>
Important reporting channels included:</p>

<ul>
<li><b>National Cyber Crime Portal:</b> https://cybercrime.gov.in</li>
<li><b>Cyber Crime Helpline:</b> 1930</li>
</ul>

<p>
This checklist helps students become more aware of online threats
and encourages responsible internet usage.
</p>
